    Research Projects

    • Heterogeneous Hypergraph Neural Network for Friend Recommendation with Human Mobility
      For human mobilities, point-of-interest (POI), time and POI type are often involved in a user check-in record. Previous works perfer classical simple graph-based methods with an edge linking two nodes that cannot fully model the complex data structure of location-based social networks. We model location-based social networks data as hyperedges in a heterogeneous LBSN hypergraph and design an end-to-end trainable heterogeneous hypergraph neural network, which can learn hypergraph node embedding for the next friend recommendation task. This paper has just been accepted by CIKM'22 as the first author. What's more, the corresponding Chinese invention patent is applying.

    • HMGCL: Heterogeneous Multigraph Contrastive Learning for LBSN Friend Recommendation
      Friend recommendation is a vital real-world application of location-based social networks (LBSN) services. We leverage heterogeneous multigraph to model human mobilities and social networks, and propose a novel Heterogeneous Multigraph Contrastive Learning model to capture spatio-temporal information and semantic information in LBSN data. This paper has just been accepted by World Wide Web Journal as the first author. What's more, the corresponding 《Friend Recommendation Methods, Devices, Apparatus and Storage Media》 has been issued as a Chinese invention patent.
